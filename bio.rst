

Leo Brodie has been thinking professionally about
software development since 1980, when he joined Forth, Inc. as a
technical writer. He has also served as a consultant, author, developer
and program manager for companies including IBM, NCR, Digalog Corp.,
Microsoft, and Real Networks. He is also the owner of Punch & Brodie
productions, which occasionally provides puppet characters for corporate
videos and company meetings. He is married and the father of three
children. He also authored *Starting Forth* (Prentice-Hall, 1981).
